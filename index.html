üßπ Cleaning & Maintenance

CRITICAL: Daily cleaning is NON-NEGOTIABLE! Prevents 90% of problems.

3-Minute Daily Cleaning

  1. Wait for "Ready to Clean" (below 150¬∞F)
  2. Remove wire rack
  3. Spray cavity with TurboChef cleaner
  4. Let sit 30 seconds
  5. Wipe with damp cloth
  6. Remove & rinse bottom jetplate (lift straight up)
  7. Rinse grease filter (gentle - no scrubbing!)
  8. Reinstall everything
  9. Spray Oven Guard lightly
  10. Close door - Done!

üîÑ Understanding Rack Oscillation

What Does RACK ON/OFF Mean?

RACK ON = Moving/Oscillating

  • Rack moves slowly side-to-side
  • 2.5" total movement
  • 8-second cycle time
  • Ensures even exposure to air jets

RACK OFF = Stationary

  • Rack stays completely still
  • Food remains in one position
  • Creates "leopard spots"
  • Rarely used (only for melting)
BOTTOM LINE: Leave RACK ON for 99% of cooking. It's essential for even browning!

üç≥ Pan vs Directly on Rack

‚úÖ Directly on Rack (BEST)

  • Turkey legs
  • Whole potatoes
  • Egg rolls

Benefits: Fastest cooking, best browning, even cooking all around

‚ö†Ô∏è When to Use a Pan

  • Items in sauce/liquid
  • Raw bacon (grease management)

Note: Add 15-25% more cook time with pan

üí® Smoke When Opening - Is It Normal?

‚úÖ Normal Smoke

  • Light wisp when door opens
  • Fatty proteins (turkey legs)
  • High-fat frozen items
  • Items at 475-500¬∞F
  • First few uses if new

‚ö†Ô∏è NOT Normal

  • Thick billowing smoke
  • Smoke during entire cycle
  • Acrid/burning smell
  • Smoke with lean foods
#1 CAUSE OF EXCESS SMOKE: Dirty oven! Daily cleaning is REQUIRED.

üßà Oil & Butter Usage Guide

‚ùå NEVER SPRAY OIL DURING COOKING!
Interior is 475-500¬∞F - oil spray can ignite!
Opening door stops cycle and loses heat.

‚úÖ Correct Way to Use Oil/Butter

  1. BEFORE cooking: Spray or brush food at prep station
  2. Best oils: High-heat cooking spray (avocado, canola)
  3. For browning: Light coating only - excess causes smoke

Which Foods Need Oil?

  • Egg rolls: Essential for golden color
  • Potato skins: For crispiness
  • Salmon: Prevents sticking
  • Turkey legs: Golden crispy skin
  • Frozen fries: Already have oil - DON'T add more!
